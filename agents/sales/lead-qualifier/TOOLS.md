# Lead Qualifier Bot — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive raw leads from CMO and Sales Lead
- Route qualified leads to Sales Lead with handoff notes
- Report qualification metrics

### Task Queue
- Pick up lead qualification tasks
- Report completion with score and routing decision

### Logger
- Log all qualification decisions with rationale
- Track lead sources and volumes

### Business Knowledge
- Load ICP criteria for specific clients
- Reference qualification frameworks
- Access lead scoring models

## Qualification Operations

### Lead Scoring
- ICP fit assessment
- Intent signal analysis
- Budget qualification
- Timeline assessment
- Decision authority check

### Lead Enrichment
- Company research
- Contact verification
- Recent activity analysis
- Competitive landscape check

### Routing
- Qualified lead handoff to Sales Lead
- Disqualification with documented rationale
- Edge case flagging for manual review
