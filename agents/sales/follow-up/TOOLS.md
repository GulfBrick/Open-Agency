# Follow-Up Bot — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive follow-up assignments from Sales Lead
- Escalate re-engagement signals and prospect replies
- Report cadence performance metrics

### Task Queue
- Pick up scheduled follow-up tasks
- Queue future touchpoints based on cadence
- Report completion with engagement data

### Logger
- Log all touchpoints with outcome
- Track sequence progress per prospect

### Business Knowledge
- Load prospect context for personalisation
- Reference previous conversation history
- Access approved messaging templates

## Follow-Up Operations

### Sequence Management
- Execute multi-step nurture cadences
- Adjust timing based on engagement
- Pause/resume sequences as needed
- Track sequence completion rates

### Engagement Tracking
- Monitor response signals
- Track email opens and clicks
- Detect re-engagement patterns
- Score engagement levels over time

### Re-Engagement
- Identify cold leads for re-activation
- Select appropriate re-engagement approach
- Deliver value-add content to dormant prospects
- Measure reactivation success rates
