# Closer Bot — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive deal assignments from Sales Lead
- Report conversation outcomes and deal progress
- Request proposals from Proposal Bot
- Escalate complex situations to Sales Lead

### Task Queue
- Pick up assigned sales conversations
- Report task completion with outcome data

### Logger
- Log all prospect interactions
- Record objections encountered and responses used

### Business Knowledge
- Load prospect and client context before conversations
- Reference case studies and success stories
- Access approved pricing and terms

## Sales Operations

### Conversation Management
- Discovery call frameworks
- Demo and presentation delivery
- Objection handling playbooks
- Close techniques and timing

### Deal Progression
- Move deals through pipeline stages
- Record decision-maker engagement
- Track buying signals and concerns
- Schedule and confirm next steps
