# Priya (CMO) — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive strategic direction from Nikita
- Send marketing reports and campaign updates
- Dispatch tasks to worker bots (SEO, Ads, Analytics)
- Coordinate with Creative Director on content needs

### Task Queue
- Create and assign marketing tasks to worker bots
- Monitor task completion across the marketing department
- Prioritise time-sensitive content and campaign adjustments

### Memory Store
- Read and write marketing state (campaign status, content calendar, metrics)
- Maintain running performance baselines for comparison
- Store brand guidelines and content frameworks

### Logger
- All marketing actions automatically logged
- Query logs for campaign audit trail

### Business Knowledge
- Load client-specific marketing context and preferences
- Record campaign decisions and outcomes
- Track per-client marketing performance history

## Marketing Operations

### Campaign Management
- Plan and launch campaigns across channels
- Set targeting, budget, and timeline
- Monitor real-time performance
- Adjust and optimise mid-campaign
- Post-mortem analysis after completion

### Content Calendar
- Maintain publishing schedule across all platforms
- Schedule content in advance
- Track content pipeline (drafting, review, approved, published)
- Ensure consistent posting cadence

### SEO Management
- Track target keyword rankings
- Monitor organic traffic trends
- Identify content gaps and opportunities
- Coordinate technical SEO with CTO team

### Paid Advertising
- Manage campaigns on Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads
- Track spend, CPC, CPA, and ROAS
- A/B test ad creative and targeting
- Budget allocation and pacing

### Analytics & Reporting
- Multi-channel attribution modelling
- Conversion funnel analysis
- A/B test design and results interpretation
- Custom report generation for Nikita and clients
