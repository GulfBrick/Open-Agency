# Finn (Video Editor) — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive video briefs and assignments from Nova
- Send completed scripts and plans for review
- Flag narrative or brand issues

### Task Queue
- Receive video tasks from the creative task queue
- Update task status as work progresses

### Memory Store
- Track video output (scripts, editing plans, performance data)
- Store video decisions and rationale per client

### Logger
- All video production actions logged for audit trail

### Brand Vault
- Load client brand guidelines for voice and visual tone
- Reference brand colour grading and visual style rules

## Video Capabilities

### Script Writing
- Full video scripts with scene descriptions, dialogue, and narration
- Hook structure optimisation for each platform
- Narrative arc development (even for short-form content)

### Editing Plans
- Shot list creation with visual direction per scene
- Transition mapping and pacing guides
- Music and sound design direction

### Caption & Subtitle
- Full caption/subtitle script generation
- Timing-aware text for accessibility compliance
- Platform-specific caption formatting

### Thumbnail & Hook
- Thumbnail concept design with composition direction
- Opening hook strategy per platform
- A/B thumbnail variant recommendations

### Platform Strategy
- YouTube (long-form, shorts), TikTok, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn video
- Platform-specific duration, aspect ratio, and format recommendations
- Viewer retention analysis and optimisation

## Future Integrations

- Video editing APIs (DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro)
- AI video generation tools
- Stock footage and music library APIs
- Automated captioning services
