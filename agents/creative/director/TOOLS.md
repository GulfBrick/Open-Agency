# Nova (Creative Director) — Tools & Integrations

## Core Backend Tools

### Message Bus
- Receive campaign briefs and strategic direction from Priya (CMO)
- Send creative reviews, approvals, and feedback to the team
- Dispatch creative tasks to worker bots (Iris, Finn, Jade, Ash)
- Report creative performance to Nikita and Priya

### Task Queue
- Create and assign creative tasks to worker bots
- Monitor task completion across the creative department
- Prioritise time-sensitive creative reviews and campaign assets

### Memory Store
- Read and write creative state (briefs, reviews, approvals, team workload)
- Maintain brand guidelines per client via Brand Vault
- Store creative performance baselines for comparison

### Logger
- All creative actions automatically logged
- Query logs for creative review audit trail

### Business Knowledge
- Load client-specific brand context and creative preferences
- Record creative decisions and outcomes
- Track per-client creative quality history

## Creative Operations

### Brief Management
- Write creative briefs from campaign strategy
- Define objectives, audience, tone, deliverables, and constraints
- Assign briefs to the appropriate creative worker bot
- Track brief completion and revision history

### Creative Review
- Review all creative assets against brief and brand guidelines
- Provide structured feedback: what works, what doesn't, and why
- Approve or request revisions with specific action items
- Track revision cycles and approval rates

### Brand Management
- Maintain brand guidelines per client in the Brand Vault
- Audit live content for brand consistency
- Update brand guidelines when approved by Nikita
- Ensure all creative output aligns with client brand identity

### Campaign Creative
- Review campaign creative performance data from Priya
- Recommend creative refreshes based on engagement metrics
- Coordinate asset production timelines with campaign launch dates

## Worker Bot Dispatch

### Iris (Graphic Designer)
- Visual concept creation and specification
- Asset production (social graphics, ads, presentations)
- Brand consistency review of visual output
- Colour palette and typography recommendations

### Finn (Video Editor)
- Video script writing and editing plans
- Caption and subtitle generation
- Thumbnail concept design
- Platform-specific video strategy

### Jade (Social Media Manager)
- Platform-native post writing
- Content calendar creation and management
- Engagement analysis and community response
- Trend monitoring and hashtag strategy

### Ash (Copywriter)
- Headline and copy writing for all formats
- CTA creation and A/B variant writing
- Email sequence and nurture copy
- Ad copy optimised for each platform
